No Mud, No Midi-chlorians: Buddhism and Star Wars

Summary:


This piece explores the philosophical parallels and differences between Buddhist teachings and the worldview presented in Star Wars. While Star Wars appears to draw heavily from Eastern philosophy, particularly Buddhism, it often misinterprets key ideas such as non-attachment, balance, and the nature of the self. By comparing concepts like the Force, the Jedi and Sith codes, and character arcs such as Anakin Skywalker and Revan, this discussion reveals how Star Wars reflects a distorted but fascinating version of Buddhist thought.
